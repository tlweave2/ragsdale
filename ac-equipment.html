Central Valley Climate Considerations

Our region's extreme heat demands properly sized and installed air conditioning:

  • Systems must handle 100°F+ temperatures for months
  • Proper insulation and duct sealing are critical
  • SEER ratings of 16+ provide best value in our climate
  • Two-stage or variable-speed systems offer superior comfort
  • Adequate airflow prevents system strain
