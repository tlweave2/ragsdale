Current Open Positions

HVAC Technicians

We're seeking experienced and entry-level HVAC technicians to join our service team.

Responsibilities:

  • Install, maintain, and repair heating and air conditioning systems
  • Diagnose system issues and provide solutions
  • Deliver exceptional customer service
  • Maintain clean, professional appearance and conduct
  • Follow safety protocols and company standards

Qualifications:

  • EPA certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Valid California driver's license with clean record
  • 1+ years HVAC experience preferred (will train right candidates)
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Physical ability to lift 50+ lbs and work in attics/crawl spaces

Customer Service Representatives

Be the friendly voice of Ragsdale's Heat & Air!

Responsibilities:

  • Answer incoming calls and schedule service appointments
  • Provide excellent customer support and follow-up
  • Coordinate technician schedules and dispatch
  • Process payments and maintain customer records
  • Handle customer inquiries and resolve concerns

Qualifications:

  • Excellent communication and phone skills
  • Proficiency with computers and scheduling software
  • Professional, friendly demeanor
  • Ability to multitask in fast-paced environment
  • HVAC industry experience a plus but not required

Comfort Consultants (Sales)

Help Central Valley families find the perfect HVAC solutions.

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct in-home consultations for system replacements
  • Assess customer needs and recommend appropriate solutions
  • Provide accurate estimates and close sales
  • Educate customers on equipment options and financing
  • Build lasting customer relationships

Qualifications:

  • Sales experience required (HVAC experience preferred)
  • Excellent interpersonal and presentation skills
  • Self-motivated with strong work ethic
  • Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
  • Ability to work evenings and weekends as needed
