Signs You Need a New Heating System

  • Furnace is 15+ years old
  • Frequent repairs becoming costly
  • Rising energy bills each winter
  • Uneven heating throughout your home
  • Yellow pilot light (carbon monoxide risk)
  • Excessive dust or dry air
  • Strange noises or burning smells
  • Your home never feels warm enough

Heating Systems We Install

Gas Furnaces

The most popular heating solution in Central Valley:

  • 80% AFUE standard efficiency models
  • 95%+ AFUE high-efficiency condensing furnaces
  • Natural gas or propane options
  • Single-stage, two-stage, or modulating burners
  • Variable-speed blower motors for enhanced comfort

Heat Pumps

Energy-efficient heating and cooling in one system:

  • All-electric operation (no gas lines needed)
  • Heats in winter, cools in summer
  • Ideal for mild Central Valley winters
  • Lower carbon footprint
  • Potential for solar power integration

    • Dual-Fuel Systems

    Best of both worlds:

    • Heat pump for mild weather
    • Gas furnace backup for cold nights
    • Maximum efficiency year-round
    • Automatic switching based on outdoor temperature

    Heating Efficiency Matters in Central Valley

    While our winters are milder than many regions, heating costs still add up:

    • Nights regularly drop to 30-40°F November through February
    • Tule fog creates damp cold that feels colder than the temperature
    • Proper sizing prevents short-cycling and wasted energy
    • High-efficiency systems pay for themselves in 5-7 years
    • Smart thermostats maximize savings and comfort
