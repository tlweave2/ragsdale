Schedule a Furnace Tune-Up with Ragsdales

Don’t skip your annual furnace tune-up! Schedule your preventive maintenance with the knowledgeable and friendly team at Ragsdales Heating, Air. 

Why should you schedule a tune-up for your heating system?

Regular tune-ups for your home's furnace are a crucial aspect of responsible homeownership, mirroring the preventative measures taken for your car's maintenance. Just as you change the oil, monitor tire pressure, and schedule regular inspections for your vehicle to ensure optimal performance, giving your furnace the attention it deserves can translate into long-term cost savings and enhanced efficiency.

By proactively addressing issues, optimizing efficiency, and ensuring safety, you not only save money but also contribute to the long-term health and performance of your heating system.


 

Getting an annual tune-up for your heating system has many advantages:

  • Helps to ensure your furnace operates efficiently, which saves you money on utility bills

  • Enhances the safety of your heating system

  • Remedies any minor issues to help prevent major repairs down the road

  • Improves the performance of the heater, so you can be comfortable when it’s cold

  • Many manufacturers require regular maintenance by a qualified HVAC technician as a condition to maintain your warranty

 How often should I schedule a tune-up for my furnace? 

The truth is, just like any type of equipment or machinery, your furnace experiences wear and tear every time you use it. To avoid frequent repair bills, high energy bills, and sub-par heater performance, the experts at Ragsdales recommend you schedule a tune-up on your furnace once a year.
