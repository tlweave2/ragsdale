THERMOSTATS

TAKE CONTROL OF YOUR HOME COMFORT SYSTEM

As suppliers of the world's leading manufacturer of thermostats (Honeywell), Ragsdales Heating, Air & cares about you and your comfort. Whether you are building a new home or upgrading your current heating and cooling equipment, we have the right thermostat for you.

Programmable Thermostats

A programmable thermostat adjusts the temperature automatically when you are away or asleep to conserve energy and save money on your utility bill. Ragsdales provides a range of Honeywell programmable thermostats that are very easy to use.

The average household spends more than $2,200 a year on energy bills — nearly half of which goes to heating and cooling. Homeowners can save about $180 a year by properly setting their programmable thermostat and maintaining those settings.

Non-Programmable Thermostats

A non-programmable thermostat is a simple set-it-and-forget-it temperature control. For those that are less concerned with energy savings, Ragsdales also offers a wide range of non-programmable Honeywell thermostats of both digital and non-digital designs.

Contact us online for more information on our thermostat offerings and how they can save you money and improve your home's comfort.
